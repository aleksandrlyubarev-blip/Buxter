Build a parametric Fusion 360 model of the Miranda RFV 2U central
liquid-cooling MANIFOLD (deliverable #3 of 10).

Fixed parameters (register all as UserParameters; reference
samples/miranda-rfv-2u/params.yaml -> manifold:):

- Material (visual only): stainless steel AISI 304, opaque grey.
- Body: 30 mm (X, across rack) x 200 mm (Y, along rack) x 30 mm (Z).
- Position: centered on chassis X (X=0), centered along Y at
  manifold_centerline_y = 350 mm from chassis front edge.
- Z bottom = 10 mm above base (clears mounting bosses).
- Body is solid stainless (we do NOT model internal supply/return
  channels in this revision; they are CFD-only).
- 4 blind-mate QDC ports, machined as cylindrical bosses on the long
  side faces (+X and -X), 12 mm long, 16 mm diameter, with a coaxial
  G1/4 thread hole (modeled as 11.0 mm clearance for the threaded
  insert; threads themselves not modeled).
  * 2 supply ports (upper, Z = 22 mm) — one on +X face, one on -X face.
  * 2 return ports (lower, Z = 8 mm) — one on +X face, one on -X face.
  * Y position of supply/return pair: ±qdc_pitch_y/2 = ±40 mm from the
    manifold center along Y.
- 2 rear-facing inlet/outlet bosses on the rear face (+Y), 12 mm
  diameter clearance hole, 12 mm boss length. One supply (upper,
  Z = 22 mm), one return (lower, Z = 8 mm), centered on X.
- 2 mounting brackets (separate components attached to the manifold
  body) on the underside, M4 clearance holes (4.5 mm), pitch 120 mm
  along Y, 4 holes total, for fastening to chassis base.
- Add a 30 x 15 mm RFID landing pad recessed 0.4 mm into the top face,
  centered.
- Add an alignment pin notch (4.0 mm dia x 5 mm deep blind hole) on the
  underside near each bracket — mates with chassis base alignment pins.

Geometry rules:

1. Build manifold body as one component named "Manifold". Add the two
   brackets as separate child components "Bracket_Front" and
   "Bracket_Rear" — they are fastened separately and must be removable.
2. Coordinate origin: chassis origin (center front edge of base, +Y
   into the rack, +Z up). Position the manifold so its body center is
   at (0, manifold_centerline_y, body_h/2 + bottom_clearance).
3. Use UserParameters for all dimensions (body_w, body_d, body_h,
   centerline_y, qdc_supply_z, qdc_return_z, qdc_pitch_y, qdc_boss_d,
   qdc_boss_l, qdc_thread_clearance, rear_inlet_d, mount_pitch,
   mount_hole_d, bottom_clearance, rfid_pad_W, rfid_pad_D,
   rfid_pad_recess, align_pin_d).

Exports — only when env vars are set:

- BUXTER_F3D, BUXTER_STEP, BUXTER_STL via design.exportManager.

Do NOT close the document automatically. Wrap run() in try/except and
surface failures via ui.messageBox(traceback.format_exc()), then
re-raise.
