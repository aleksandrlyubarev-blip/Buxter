L-shaped mounting bracket, 60 mm horizontal flange × 40 mm vertical flange,
both flanges 30 mm wide and 4 mm thick. Outer corner radius 2 mm, inner
fillet 1 mm. Two 4.3 mm clearance holes (for M4 bolts) on the horizontal
flange, spaced 40 mm along the flange centreline, 15 mm from the corner.
One 4.3 mm clearance hole in the middle of the vertical flange. Flat base
face for bed adhesion. Units millimetres.
